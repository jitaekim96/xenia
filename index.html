What is healthius?

Healthius aims to crowdsource available resources to alleviate food insecurity by utilizing human-centered planning algorithms. By doing so, we work towards efficiently communicating the needs of the food insecure population and empowering donors.

Voices

Voices, an iPad app, empowers food pantry clients to voice their input on what they can't find in their pantries. This feedback ultimately helps pantry managers and donors to better understand the food-related needs of the food insecure population.

Healthius

Healthius is a web platform that visualizes collected data to better inform stakeholders involved in community service and empowers donors to donate to causes they are passionate for.
